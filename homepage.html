 About Me

My name is Kent Pendergast and I am currently a senior at Babson College. I chose Babson because I found it to be the best fit for me both for the education and the small campus. Growing up in NYC, I watched my father start his own business and hoped that someday I would be able to do the same thing. Because of these things, Babson was a strong contender in my college selection.

After graduation, I would like to pursue a job in the corporate finance or venture capital field. I have enjoyed the analysis of companies through my finance concentration at Babson.
